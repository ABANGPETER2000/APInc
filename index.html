ABOUT AFRIK-NAIJA PRODUCTION INC.
Our Experience

With over 20 years of experience in the building industry, Afrik-Naija Production Inc has established itself as a reliable and trusted contractor in the region. Our team has worked on a variety of projects, from small residential renovations to large-scale commercial constructions.

Our Commitment to Quality
At Afrik-Naija Production Inc, we are committed to delivering high-quality workmanship on every project. We use only the best materials and equipment, and we ensure that our team is up-to-date with the latest building techniques and industry standards.

Our Client Satisfaction

Our clients' satisfaction is our top priority. We work closely with them to ensure that their vision is fully realised and that the project is completed on time and within budget. We strive to exceed their expectations and to build long-lasting relationships.

OUR SERVICES

We offer full-service general contracting, specializing in site development and fencing for private, commercial, and municipal projects. Our expertise includes project management, sound barrier walls, fencing, carpentry, site preparation, earthworks, civil infrastructure, parks, hardscape, and softscape.
